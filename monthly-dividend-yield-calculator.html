FAQ

What is a “good” dividend yield?

It depends on the investment. Many people plan with a range like 3%–7%, but higher yields can mean higher risk. Use this calculator to test different income and investment scenarios.

Why show “monthly yield”?

Most dividend yields are quoted annually. We show an equivalent monthly yield for planning only (annual yield divided by 12).
