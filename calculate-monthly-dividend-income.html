FAQ

Why is yield annual if I want monthly income?

Yield is usually quoted per year. This tool calculates annual income first, then divides by 12 for a monthly estimate. If an investment pays quarterly, you may receive larger payments four times a year, but the yearly total is what the yield represents.

Does this include dividend growth or DRIP?

No—this page is a quick estimator. If you want growth and reinvestment modeling, use the main calculator.
